Soccer: The World's Sport

Soccer is one of if not the most popular sports across the globe. Children, parents, and adults everywhere can be seen playing the sport. Soccer has always been popular in countries such as Argentina or Spain, but the popularity is just starting to grow here in the United States. Because of that, this site was created to inform the every-day American Joe the basics of soccer, including top performing players and popular tournaments such as the FIFA World Cup.

What is the FIFA World Cup?

The World Cup is an international soccer tournament contested by senior men’s national teams from various countries. It is one of the most popular international sporting competitions, with an estimated 1.5 billion fans tuning in to watch the 2022 final. Fans travel from all over the world to follow their favorite teams as they compete against some of the most talented players in soccer. The World Cup is made up of teams from six different continental zones – Africa, Asia, Europe, North and Central America and the Caribbean, Oceania, and South America. There is also a Women’s Worlds of Soccer which follows a similar format. The next Women’s Worlds tournament will take place in 2027.
